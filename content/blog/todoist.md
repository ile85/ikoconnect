## ✅ What Is Todoist?

**Todoist** is a powerful yet simple task management tool used by millions to plan, organize, and prioritize daily tasks. Whether you're a freelancer juggling multiple clients or a remote worker trying to manage deadlines, Todoist is your digital to-do list for everything—from grocery shopping to major work projects.

🧠 _Used by over 30 million people worldwide to track over 1.5 billion tasks._

## 🧪 Productivity Tips with Todoist

- Use `p1` for top priorities, `p4` for low-effort fillers  
- Filter view: `Today & @work` = focus mode  
- Keyboard shortcuts: `q` for quick add  
- Plan your day every morning with the **Daily Review**  
- Batch similar tasks with `@deepwork` or `@admin`
