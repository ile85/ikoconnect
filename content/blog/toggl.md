## 🧠 How Toggl Works

1. ✅ Create a project (e.g., "Client X – Website Redesign")  
2. 🕒 Hit “Start” on a task like “Homepage UI”  
3. 📍 Stop when done, or leave it running (Toggl will remind you)  
4. 📊 Export weekly timesheets to invoice or analyze

It’s **effortless and precise**—and works on all devices (desktop, mobile, browser).
