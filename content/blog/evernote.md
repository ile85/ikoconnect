## 🧠 What is Evernote?

**Evernote** is more than a note-taking app—it's your **second brain**. Whether you're managing tasks, planning content, or storing your next big idea, Evernote provides a centralized space to organize your personal and professional life.

From freelancers to CEOs, it's the go-to app for **digital organization**.

---

## ✨ Core Features of Evernote

- ✅ **Rich Text Notes** – Format with bold, checklists, code blocks, attachments, images
- ✅ **Web Clipper** – Save web pages, articles, screenshots directly to Evernote
- ✅ **Tagging + Notebooks** – Smart organization of all content
- ✅ **Templates** – For journaling, meeting notes, project planning
- ✅ **Cross-platform Sync** – Android, iOS, macOS, Windows, Web
- ✅ **Search Inside PDFs & Handwritten Notes** (Premium)
- ✅ **Task Manager** – Add to-dos with due dates, reminders, and priorities
- ✅ **Calendar Integration** – Sync with Google Calendar
