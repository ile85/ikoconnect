## 🚀 What is HubSpot?

[HubSpot](https://www.hubspot.com/) is an all-in-one **CRM (Customer Relationship Management)** platform that empowers businesses to grow by streamlining marketing, sales, and customer support activities.

It’s trusted by startups, SMBs, and enterprises to manage their:

- Customer relationships
- Email marketing campaigns
- Sales pipelines
- Lead nurturing funnels
- Customer service tickets

Whether you're a solo freelancer or a team of 200, HubSpot scales with your needs.

## 🧪 Sample Lead Workflow

1. Visitor downloads a lead magnet on your site
2. Contact is added to HubSpot CRM
3. Lead is tagged with “Interested – Design Services”
4. Automatic welcome email is triggered
5. Sales rep is notified and assigned
6. Meeting is scheduled
7. Deal is created in pipeline

All this – no manual effort.
