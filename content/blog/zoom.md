## 📹 What is Zoom?

**Zoom** is a modern, cloud-based video conferencing and communication platform trusted by **millions of individuals, freelancers, educators, and global enterprises**.

It offers high-quality video and audio conferencing, chat, webinar hosting, and collaboration tools—all accessible from your computer, tablet, or mobile phone.

---

## 🚀 Why Zoom Stands Out

- 🧠 **Easy to use** — Join or host a meeting in seconds
- 🔒 **Secure** — End-to-end encryption and compliance tools
- 🌐 **Scalable** — From 1-on-1 calls to webinars with thousands
- ⚙️ **Versatile** — Screen sharing, breakout rooms, whiteboard, recordings

Whether you’re freelancing, teaching online, managing a remote team, or selling a service—Zoom helps you **connect and collaborate seamlessly**.
