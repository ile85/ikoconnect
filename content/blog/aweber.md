## How to Get Started with AWeber

1. **Create an Account**  
   Sign up at [aweber.com](https://www.aweber.com/) (free plan available)

2. **Create Your First List**  
   Add your brand name, email address, and email signature

3. **Build Your Welcome Email**  
   Use drag-and-drop editor or Smart Designer  
   Add logo, welcome message, and a call-to-action

4. **Set Up Automation**  
   Build a 3-part welcome series  
   Example:  
   - Day 1: Welcome  
   - Day 3: Value-packed tip  
   - Day 7: Offer or case study

5. **Create a Landing Page**  
   Use the prebuilt “Newsletter Signup” template  
   Add your lead magnet (PDF, checklist, free course, etc.)

6. **Share & Grow**  
   Share your landing page on social media, website, or link-in-bio  
   Monitor signups and refine content based on open rates
