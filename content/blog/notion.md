## 🧠 What is Notion?

[**Notion**](https://www.notion.so/) is an all-in-one workspace where you can write, plan, collaborate, and get organized. It allows individuals and teams to **create notes**, **manage projects**, **build databases**, and even run internal wikis—all inside one beautifully minimal interface.

From solo freelancers to entire enterprises, Notion has become the go-to productivity tool for **flexibility without chaos**.

## 🚀 Advanced Tips

- Use **“/template” blocks** to create reusable blocks  
- Embed **public Google Docs or Sheets** directly in Notion  
- Connect Notion with **Zapier** or **Make.com** for automation  
- Use **form tools** like Tally.so to feed into Notion  
- Create a **home dashboard** with links to everything you need daily
