## 🎯 What is Trello?

**Trello** is a visual project management and collaboration tool that helps individuals and teams organize tasks, ideas, and workflows using **boards**, **lists**, and **cards**.

Inspired by the Kanban methodology, Trello gives you a bird’s-eye view of your entire project—from ideas to “done.” Whether you're a solopreneur, part of a remote team, or a growing agency, Trello helps you stay on track.

---

## 🧠 Why Use Trello?

- ✅ Visual layout that’s intuitive for beginners

- 🔁 Drag & drop simplicity

## 💥 Trello Power-Ups

Power-Ups are plugins that **extend Trello’s functionality**.

| Power-Up | Function |
|----------|----------|
| 📆 Calendar | See cards with due dates on a calendar |
| 📊 Dashcards | Turn lists into KPIs and metrics |
| 🧠 Butler | Automate actions (e.g., "When moved to Done, mark complete") |
| 📁 Google Drive | Attach Google Docs/Sheets directly |
| 🧾 Custom Fields | Add extra data like budget, priority, etc. |
| 🗓️ Trello + Slack | Get task notifications in Slack |

Free plan includes 1 Power-Up per board, paid plans allow unlimited.

## 🔄 Pro Tips & Productivity Hacks

✅ Use labels (colors + keywords) for faster sorting  
✅ Automate routine tasks with Butler  
✅ Use checklists inside cards for sub-tasks  
✅ Turn emails into cards via Trello Email-to-Board

✅ Use the Calendar view as a mini content planner  
✅ Archive old cards to keep boards clean  
✅ Use card templates to save time
