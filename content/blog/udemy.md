## 🎓 What is Udemy?

**Udemy** is one of the world’s largest online learning platforms, offering over **200,000 courses** on topics ranging from software development and digital marketing to photography, wellness, personal finance, and more.

Whether you're a beginner wanting to learn a new skill, a freelancer upgrading your toolkit, or a professional advancing your career—**Udemy has something for everyone**.

Courses are created and taught by independent instructors, which brings **variety, creativity, and real-world experience** to the table.

- 📆 Check the **last updated** date to make sure content is recent
- 💬 Read student Q&A and reviews for extra insights
- 🏆 Check if a **certificate of completion** is included
