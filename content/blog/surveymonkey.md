## 🧪 Question Logic (Branching)

With **Skip Logic**, **Piping**, and **Conditional Branching**, you can build smart surveys that adapt to users’ answers.

> Example: If someone selects “No” for “Have you used our service?”, you can skip follow-up questions about satisfaction.

This leads to **higher completion rates** and better data.
