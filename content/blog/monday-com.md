## 🧠 What is Monday.com?

[Monday.com](https://monday.com/) is more than just project management—it's a full **Work OS (Operating System)** designed to help teams **collaborate**, **track**, and **execute** projects of all kinds, from marketing campaigns to software development sprints.

It combines **visual boards**, **automation**, **dashboards**, and **integrations** into one user-friendly platform.
