## 🎨 What is Adobe Creative Cloud?

**Adobe Creative Cloud (Adobe CC)** is a subscription-based collection of industry-leading applications for **graphic design**, **video editing**, **web development**, **photography**, and more.

It provides a full suite of professional tools for **creatives, marketers, freelancers, and entrepreneurs**, all connected via cloud services for seamless collaboration.

---

## 📦 What's Included in Creative Cloud?

Adobe CC includes **20+ apps** for desktop, web, and mobile. Some of the most popular:

| App             | Description                                                             |
|------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Photoshop**     | Industry-standard for photo editing and digital art                   |
| **Illustrator**   | Vector design for logos, icons, illustrations                         |
| **Premiere Pro**  | Professional video editing suite                                     |
| **After Effects** | Visual effects and motion graphics for video                          |
| **InDesign**      | Layout design for magazines, PDFs, brochures                          |
| **Lightroom**     | Photo editing and organization for photographers                      |
| **XD**            | UX/UI design and prototyping                                          |
| **Acrobat Pro**   | Edit, sign, and manage PDFs                                           |
| **Audition**      | Audio editing and cleanup                                             |
| **Animate**       | 2D animation for games, websites, and videos                         |

And many more, including tools for **3D**, **collaboration**, **fonts**, and **cloud storage**.
