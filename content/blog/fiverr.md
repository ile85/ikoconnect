## 📝 How Fiverr Works: Step-by-Step

### 1. Sign Up  
Go to [Fiverr](https://go.fiverr.com/visit/?bta=1031917&brand=fiverrmarketplace) and create a seller account.

### 2. Create Your Gig  
Write a compelling title like “I will design a modern logo for your business.”  
✅ Add relevant tags  
✅ Upload images or video samples  
✅ Set pricing tiers (Basic, Standard, Premium)

### 3. Get Orders  
Your gigs will be listed in Fiverr’s search results. When a client places an order, you’ll be notified via email and dashboard.

### 4. Deliver Work  
Submit files or deliverables directly through the Fiverr platform. Use revisions wisely!

### 5. Get Paid  
You receive 80% of the sale price. Payments clear 14 days after order completion.

## ✅ Best Practices for Fiverr Success

- 🎯 **Niche Down**: Don’t say “I’ll do everything.” Say “I’ll write SEO blog posts for SaaS startups.”
- 🖼️ **Gig Images Matter**: Use clean, attractive visuals
- 📹 **Add a Video**: Sellers with intro videos convert 200% better
- ⏱️ **Respond Fast**: Fiverr ranks you higher when your response time is <1 hour
- 🌟 **Ask for Reviews**: Politely request feedback after delivery
- 📈 **Use Gig Extras**: Upsell faster delivery, extra features, etc.
