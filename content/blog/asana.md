## Getting Started: Quick Setup Guide

1. **Create an Account**  
   Sign up at [asana.com](https://asana.com/) using Google or email.

2. **Create Your First Project**  
   Use a pre-made template or start blank. Choose "Board" or "List" view.

3. **Add Sections & Tasks**  
   Break your project into sections (e.g., Planning, In Progress, Completed) and populate with tasks.

4. **Assign Tasks**  
   Each task should have an assignee and due date to stay on track.

5. **Invite Your Team**  
   Invite collaborators and control their permissions (Editor, Commenter, Viewer).

6. **Set Rules or Automations**  
   Add simple automations like: "When task is completed, move to 'Done' column."

7. **Track Progress with Dashboards**  
   Visualize task completion, overdue items, and workloads using the â€œReportingâ€ tab.
