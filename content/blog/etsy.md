## 🛍️ What Is Etsy?

**Etsy** is the go-to platform for buying and selling **handmade, vintage, and craft supplies**. With over **96 million active buyers** and millions of sellers globally, Etsy empowers independent creatives to reach an audience that values authenticity, craftsmanship, and personal touches.

Unlike Amazon or eBay, Etsy focuses on **uniqueness**, **customization**, and **creativity**.

---

## 🔍 What Can You Sell on Etsy?

From art to apparel, Etsy is perfect for:

- 🖼️ **Printables & Wall Art**

- 🎨 **Craft Supplies & Tools**  
- 🧵 **Sewing Patterns & Embroidery Kits**  
- 📚 **Digital Downloads (eBooks, planners, etc.)**  
- 🪑 **Vintage Items (20+ years old)**  
- 💌 **Stationery & Custom Cards**

## 🌟 Key Features of Etsy

- **Built-in audience** of millions
- Low startup cost – ~$0.20 per listing
- Seller tools: coupons, ads, analytics
- Integrated shipping & order management
- Personal storefront (shop.etsy.com/yourname)
- Etsy Ads & offsite promotion
- Trusted payment processing via Etsy Payments

## 📊 Etsy Fees Breakdown

| Fee Type            | Amount                       |
|---------------------|------------------------------|
| Listing Fee         | $0.20 per item (4 months)    |
| Transaction Fee     | 6.5% of item price           |
| Payment Processing  | ~3% + $0.25 (varies by region) |
| Offsite Ads Fee     | 12–15% (if a sale occurs via offsite ad) |

💡 Tip: Factor these into your pricing to stay profitable.

## 📝 How to Start Your Etsy Shop (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Create Your Account  
Go to [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com/) → Click "Sell on Etsy"

### Step 2: Choose Your Shop Name  
Unique, brandable, under 20 characters

### Step 3: Add Your First Product  
- High-quality images (use mockups!)  
- Write compelling titles + SEO tags  
- Describe your item clearly (shipping, variations, etc.)

### Step 4: Set Up Billing & Payments  
Connect your bank account and set currency

### Step 5: Promote Your Listings  
- Share on social media  
- Use Etsy Ads  
- Encourage reviews for credibility
