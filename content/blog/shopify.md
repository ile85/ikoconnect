## 🛒 What is Shopify?

[**Shopify**](https://www.shopify.com/) is one of the world’s most popular platforms for building **online stores**. Whether you're selling handmade goods, digital products, or dropshipping, Shopify gives you everything you need to build, grow, and manage an e-commerce business—all in one place.

Founded in 2006, it powers over **4 million businesses** across 175 countries.
