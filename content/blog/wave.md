## 💳 Accepting Payments

Wave supports:

- Visa / Mastercard / Amex
- Bank transfers (ACH)
- Apple Pay (in some regions)

The platform charges a small fee **only when you get paid**:
- Credit card: ~2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- Bank transfer (ACH): ~1% per transaction (US)

No monthly fees. No setup costs. No contract.
